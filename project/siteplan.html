Website Planning Document

Site Name

El Jabalí Racetrack

This name represents a motorsports racetrack located in El Salvador, known as "El Jabalí." It's a name that motorsport fans in the region already recognize. If available, we could use autodromoeljabali.com as a domain.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as the official online presence of the Jabalí Racetrack. It will provide information about upcoming races and events, ticket sales, services, and contact details. The site will also promote motorsport culture and encourage more visitors and sponsors to engage with the track.

Scenarios

  • When is the next racing event at the Jabalí?
  • How can I rent the track for a private event or practice session?
  • Where can I buy tickets or see photos of past events?

Color Scheme

We’ve selected a bold and energetic color palette to match the motorsport theme:

Primary: #FF6B6B – Used for buttons, call-to-action, highlights.
Secondary: #FFE66D – Used for secondary buttons, navigation hovers.
Background: #F7FFF7 – Used as the page background color.
Heading Text: #1A535C – Used for titles and main headings.
Body Text: #4E4E4E – Used for paragraphs and general text.
Accent: #4ECDC4 – Used for links, hover states, small UI elements.

Typography

  • Headings: 'Oswald', sans-serif – A strong, modern font suitable for motorsport branding.
  • Body text: 'Roboto', sans-serif – Clean and highly readable, ideal for paragraphs and longer text.
